## 1.6*
 The solutions to the quadratic equation x^2 - 11x + 22 = 0 are x = 3 and x = 6. What is the base of the numbers?

 $$
 3 \times 3 - 11 \times 3 + 22 = 0 \\
 9 - (b + 1 \times 3) + 22 = 0 \\
 9 - 3b - 3 + 2b + 2 = 0 \\
 - 3b + 2b + 9 - 3 + 2 = 0 \\
 -b + 6 + 2 = 0  \\
 -b + 8 = 0  \\
 b = 8  \\
 $$

 
 $$
 6 \times 6 - 11 \times 6 + 22 = 0 \\
 36 - (b + 1 \times 6) + 22 = 0 \\
 36 - 6b - 6 + 2b + 2 = 0 \\
 -4b + 36 -6 + 2 = 0 \\
 -4b  + 36 - 4 = 0 \\
 -4b + 32 = 0  \\
 4b = 32  \\
 4b \div 4 = 32 \div 4 \\
 b = 8
 $$

 ## 1.8
 Convert the decimal number 431 to binary in two ways: (a) convert directly to binary;
(b) convert first to hexadecimal and then from hexadecimal to binary. Which method is faster?

(a)
$$
431 \div 2 = 215 r 1 \\
215 \div 2 = 107 r 1 \\
107 \div 2 = 53  r 1 \\
53  \div 2 = 26  r 1 \\
26  \div 2 = 13  r 0 \\
13  \div 2 = 6   r 1 \\
6   \div 2 = 3   r 0 \\
3   \div 2 = 1   r 1 \\
1   \div 2 = 0   r 1 \\
\\[1em]
Result = (1\ 1100\ 0011)_{2} \\
$$

(b)
$$
431 \div 16 = 26 r 15 = F \\
26  \div 16 = 1  r 10 = A \\
1   \div 16 = 0  r 1  = 1 \\

Hex = (1AF)_{16} \\
\\[2em]
\text {Hex to binary:} \\
\\[1em]
Fh = 1111b \\
Ah = 1010b \\
1h = 0001b \\
\\[1em]
Result = (0001\ 1100\ 0011)_{2} = \\
(1\ 1100\ 0011)_{2}
$$

(a) is more straightforward, (b) has possibly less steps

$$

Result = 10000

Multiplication:

Rules
$$
0 \times 0 = 0 \\
0 \times 1 = 0 \\

Multiply each bit with the number and shift to the left, and then perform binary addition
```
       1011
 x      101
       ----
       1011
      0000
 +   1011
     ------
     110111
```
Result = 110111

**(b) Hexadecimal numbers 2E and 34.**

Addition:

```
Carry     1
       -----
          2E
       +  34
       -----
```

(Eh + 4h = 12h, carry 1)

```
   2E

  ---

+  80
  ---
```

In other words:
